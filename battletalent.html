What Is Battle Talent

Battle Talent is a roguelite VR action game, brings you high quality Fight, Build and Run experiences.

Game Features

Fight in your way

you have infinit possibilities in each move with our advanced physics simulation.

Build in your way

archmage? swordmaster? marksman? the choice is yours!

Run in your way
